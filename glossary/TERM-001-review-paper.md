# TERM-001 — Review Paper

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| ID | TERM-001 |
| Date Added | 2026-W29 |

## Definition (in my own words)

A paper written to survey and summarize the existing state of a research area, rather than to propose a new method or run new comparative experiments.

## Formal Definition / Source

Faculty advisor, [MEET-002](../meetings/MEET-002-research-areas-and-paper-categories.md): purpose is to understand the research landscape.

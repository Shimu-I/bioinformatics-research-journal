# TERM-003 — Methodology Paper

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| ID | TERM-003 |
| Date Added | 2026-W29 |

## Definition (in my own words)

A paper whose purpose is to propose a new method for solving a problem, typically including an implementation and experimental results demonstrating its performance.

## Formal Definition / Source

Faculty advisor, [MEET-002](../meetings/MEET-002-research-areas-and-paper-categories.md): purpose is to propose a new method.

## Example

[PAPER-001](../01-papers/PAPER-001-mirna-disease-baseline-candidate.md), a methodology paper for miRNA–Disease Association Prediction, selected as a candidate baseline and later rejected on reproducibility grounds.

## Topics Discussed

- Correct order of operations when starting a new research topic

## Advice Given

The correct workflow is: choose a topic, study several papers, select a reproducible baseline, reproduce the work, understand the methodology deeply, and only then identify limitations and propose novelty. Novelty should not be considered before a baseline is selected and reproduced.

- Criteria for selecting a paper to reproduce
- Reproducibility problems found in PAPER-001's repository
- Three categories of research papers: review, benchmark, methodology

## Advice Given

- Choose papers that provide source code, datasets, and reproducible experiments.
- Reject PAPER-001 and move on rather than continuing to invest time reconstructing a poorly documented pipeline.
- Review papers exist to understand the research landscape; benchmark papers exist to compare multiple methods fairly; methodology papers exist to propose a new method.

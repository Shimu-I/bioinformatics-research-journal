## The Question

Given a Computer Science / Data Science background rather than a biology background, which bioinformatics subfield should I focus on, and how should I approach learning the field so that background gap doesn't block progress?

## Motivation

Early exploration of review and foundation papers surfaced far more biological background than I currently have, making it unclear whether to invest time mastering biology first or to find an approach that plays to existing strengths.

## Current Understanding

Learning is more effective when approached from a computational and machine learning perspective rather than trying to master every biological concept upfront. Faculty guidance narrowed the subfield search to areas with strong ML relevance; **miRNA–Disease Association Prediction** was selected as the working topic (see [DEC-003](../04-decisions/DEC-003-select-mirna-disease-association-topic.md)).

# PRIN-001 — Always select and reproduce a baseline before considering novelty

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| ID | PRIN-001 |
| Date Established | 2026-W28 |
| Status | Active |

## Statement

Never consider novelty or contribution ideas before a reproducible baseline has been selected, reproduced, and deeply understood.

## Origin

- [MIST-001 — Considered novelty before selecting a reproducible baseline](../mistakes/MIST-001-novelty-before-baseline.md)
- [MEET-001 — Correction: baseline before novelty](../../meetings/MEET-001-baseline-before-novelty-correction.md)

## Explanation

Novelty claims are unfounded without first knowing, in detail, what already works. Reproducing and understanding a baseline is what makes it possible to identify genuine limitations rather than assumed ones.

## When to Apply

At the start of every new topic or paper search — before spending time thinking about what could be improved or contributed.

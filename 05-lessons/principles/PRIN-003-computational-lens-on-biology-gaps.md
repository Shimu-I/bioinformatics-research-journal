# PRIN-003 — Approach biological gaps through a computational/ML lens, not by front-loading biology

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| ID | PRIN-003 |
| Date Established | 2026-W28 |
| Status | Active |

## Statement

When a biology background gap appears, close it as needed for the specific problem at hand through a computational/ML framing, rather than pausing to master the underlying biology comprehensively first.

## Origin

- [DEC-001 — Approach bioinformatics from a computational/ML perspective rather than biology-mastery-first](../../04-decisions/DEC-001-computational-ml-first-approach.md)

## Explanation

Existing strength is in Data Science and Machine Learning. Progress is faster and more sustainable leaning on that strength and filling biology gaps on demand, rather than treating biology mastery as a prerequisite.

## When to Apply

Whenever a paper or task surfaces unfamiliar biological concepts — look up only what's needed to proceed, and move on.

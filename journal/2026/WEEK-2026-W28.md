## Reflections

The biggest realization this week wasn't technical — it was about approach. Trying to absorb biology at the same depth as the papers assume was slowing everything down, and it made more sense to treat biological knowledge as something to acquire on demand rather than a prerequisite. The novelty mistake was a useful early correction: it's easy to want to jump to "what can I contribute" before actually knowing what already exists and works.
